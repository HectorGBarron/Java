Recipe for Onion Soup
 
	 Instructions 
Butter and flour a 9 inch round pan.  Peel and slice pears.  Blend eggs, sugar, milk, and salt.  Add flour, mix well.  Fold 1/2 of pears into batter.  Pour into pan, fan remaining pears on top.  Dot with butter.  Bake at 350 
 
	Ingredients 

Onion 1.0 piece
Tomato 1.0 piece
Ingredient 3 1.0 piece
