Butter and flour a 9 inch round pan.  Peel and slice pears.  Blend eggs, sugar, milk, and salt.  Add flour, mix well.  Fold 1/2 of pears into batter.  Pour into pan, fan remaining pears on top.  Dot with butter.  Bake at 350 degrees about 55 minutes.  Sprinkle with powdered sugar.
butter 2 tbps
flour 1.5 cups
pears 3 pieces
eggs 2 pieces
